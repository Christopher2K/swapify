# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: November 29, 2024_

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Swapify ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we
collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our music
library transfer service between Spotify and Apple Music (the "Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

- Email address
- Username
- Password (encrypted)
- Authentication tokens for Spotify and Apple Music
- IP address and device information

### 2.2 Music Service Data

When you use our Service, we collect:

- Playlist information
- Music library contents
- Transfer history
- Music service connection status

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To create and maintain your Swapify account
- To authenticate your identity
- To connect with your music service accounts (Spotify and Apple Music)
- To perform music library transfers
- To maintain transfer history
- To provide customer support
- To send service-related communications
- To improve our services
- To detect and prevent fraud

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage

- Your personal information is stored on secure servers hosted by
  [Railway](https://railway.app)
- Music library, playlist data and transfer history is maintained for service
  improvement and user reference

### 4.2 Security Measures

- All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
- We use SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- Regular security audits and updates are performed
- Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Music Services

We interact with:

- Spotify API
- Apple Music API

These interactions are necessary for the core functionality of our Service. We
only access the data necessary to perform transfers and maintain connection
status.

### 5.2 Data Processing Services

We may use third-party services for:

- Cloud storage
- Analytics
- Customer support
- Email communication

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Revoke music service permissions
- Export your data
- Opt-out of non-essential communications

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

- Account data: As long as your account is active
- Transfer history: 12 months after transfer completion
- Music service tokens: Until you revoke access
- Usage logs: 90 days

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly
collect information from children under 13.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred and processed in countries other than your
own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any
material changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy on our website
- Sending an email to registered users
- Displaying a notice in our application

## 11. Contact Information

## 14. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA, including:

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

- Notify affected users within 72 hours

- Outline steps we're taking to address the situation
